Oracle Global Leaders Worldwide Meeting 2017

 


 Dear Oracle Global Leaders members, prospective members, Oracle Partners and Colleagues, 

 

You're invited to our annual worldwide gathering at Oracle OpenWorld 2017 which will give a round up of announcements at OW in the cloud and big data management space and honor winners of 3 Excellence Awards for 4 regions. These awards are the following categories:

  • Cloud Architect of the Year Award
  • DBA of the Year Award
  • DW & Big Data Leader of the Year Award

 

Some of the winners of this years awards will speak about what their companies have been doing and why they have won.

 

This event will take place from 10am on Thursday 5th October, with light lunch and bar reception following the meeting at the Westin St Francis Hotel on Union Square.
